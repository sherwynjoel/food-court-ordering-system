# User Manual: Centralized Food Court Ordering System

## For Super Admins
- **Login**: Use administrative credentials.
- **Branches**: Create new food court branches and manage their settings.
- **Subscriptions**: Manage which branches are active.

## For Branch Admins
- **Kitchens**: Add kitchen stalls present in your branch.
- **Users**: Create accounts for kitchen staff and branch managers.
- **Menu Management**: Upload menu items, set prices, and assign them to specific kitchens.

## For Kitchen Staff (KDS)
1. Open the **Kitchen Display** page.
2. Orders will appear in the **PENDING** column automatically.
3. Click "Start Cooking" to move an order to **PREPARING**.
4. Click "Mark Ready" to alert the customer.
5. Click "Complete" once the order is picked up.

## For Customers
1. Scan the QR code at your table (or visit the URL).
2. Browse the menu combined from all kitchens.
3. Add items to your cart.
4. Checkout to place your order.
5. Track your order status in real-time on the status page.
6. Print your receipt if needed.
